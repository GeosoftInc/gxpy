Get the voxel size in ground units that has non-dummy data.

Available since Oasis montaj version: 9.5
License: Available to anyone.  

Namespace: GeoEngine.Core.GXNet
Assembly: geoengine.core.gxnet (in geoengine.core.gxnet.dll)
Notes
Find the non-dummy volume of a MULTIGRID3D object. If the voxel is all dummies, returns &lt;define&gt;iMAX&lt;/define&gt; for the minima, and &lt;define&gt;iMIN&lt;/define&gt; for the maxima.
