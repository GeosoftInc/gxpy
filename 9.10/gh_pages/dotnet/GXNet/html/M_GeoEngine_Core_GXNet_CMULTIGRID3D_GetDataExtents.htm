Get the voxel size that has non-dummy data.

Find the non-dummy volume of a MULTIGRID3D object. If the voxel is all dummies, returns &lt;define&gt;iMAX&lt;/define&gt; for the minima, and &lt;define&gt;iMIN&lt;/define&gt; for the maxima.
