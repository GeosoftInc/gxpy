Clips a line in or out of the polygons for intersections (&lt;define&gt;GS_DOUBLE&lt;/define&gt;). Intersections are returned as fiducials down the line stored in VV starting at the first point of the line. Examples: No intersection: &lt;define&gt;PLY_LINE_CLIP_OUTSIDE&lt;/define&gt;, 0 intersections Starts outside, ends inside: &lt;define&gt;PLY_LINE_CLIP_OUTSIDE&lt;/define&gt;, 1 intersection Starts outside, intersects then ends inside or outside: &lt;define&gt;PLY_LINE_CLIP_OUTSIDE&lt;/define&gt;, 2 intersections Starts inside, ends inside : &lt;define&gt;PLY_LINE_CLIP_INSIDE&lt;/define&gt;, 1 intersection (gives end-of-line) Starts inside, ends outside : &lt;define&gt;PLY_LINE_CLIP_INSIDE&lt;/define&gt;, 1 intersection
