classmethod cross_correlation_pg(pg1, pg2, normalise, pgo)[source]¶

Calculate the cross-correlation of two transform pagers: The complex conjugate of (r, i) items in the first pager are multiplied by the second pager (r, i) values.

Parameters:

  • pg1 (GXPG) – Input pager 1

  • pg2 (GXPG) – Input pager 2

  • normalise (int) – 0 - do not normalise, 1 - result is normalised by max value

  • pgo (GXPG) – Output pager

New in version 2023.2.
